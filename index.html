I am from Elko, Nevada... a small rural town in the Northeast corner of Nevada (far from Vegas). I will be returning to Elko in December to be a graphic and event designer. I will also be doing floral design for a local floral studio, which is something I enjoy. After I complete my degree in May, I'm looking to get a UX design position in Salt Lake City, Utah. I enjoy a wide variety of activities including traveling, creating, soccer, hiking, fishing, kayaking, finding new coffee shops, cooking, and doing freelance interior design. My Mom and I recently launched our first business together, Ophelia Flower Farm. We live out in the country on three acres, so we decided to transform a section of our property into a flower farm! We grow over 50 varieties of flowers in various colors. Starting our flower farm has been a blast (& a lot of work) and it has went over really well in our community! I played soccer for sixteen years, which is one of the reasons I went to Bob Jones! I got to be a Bruin for a year before a knee and hip injury ended my soccer career. When I return to Elko I'm hoping to get involved with the youth by assisting with coaching the club soccer team! I'm looking forward to using what I learned at Bob Jones to serve my community and any other community I end up in!
